### 3. Supply Chain Risk & Performance Analytics
End-to-end R analytics project analyzing 180,519 global supply chain orders (2015–2018). Conducted data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), executive-level visualizations, and logistic regression modeling to identify operational drivers of late delivery risk and profitability across five global markets.

**Key Findings:**
- First Class shipping carries the highest late delivery risk across all markets
- Shipping mode is a stronger predictor of delivery risk than geography or customer segment
- Logistic regression achieved 69.54% classification accuracy
- Fishing, Cleats, and Camping & Hiking are the highest-profit product categories

**Business Recommendation:** Prioritize operational improvements for First Class fulfillment and deploy predictive analytics within order management systems to proactively identify high-risk shipments before dispatch.

**Tools:** R | ggplot2 | dplyr | lubridate | treemapify | caret | Logistic Regression
**Repository:** supply-chain-risk-analytics/
